Philadelphia

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania is a wonderful place to visit. From historical landmarks to modern coffee shops, there is something for everyone.


Landmarks

Philadelphia has various historical landmarks.

  • You can visit Independence Hall, where the Declaration of Independence was signed in 1776.
  • Another great place to visit the Betsy Ross House, where the American flag was first created.
  • A must-see when visiting Philadelphia is the Liberty Bell.

Transportation

Philadelphia has a multitude of means of transportation.

Philadelphia is the most walkable city in the United States. While you can walk anywhere, there are multiple means of getting around the city.
Many people rely on the SEPTA to get around. There are SEPTA buses, and there are underground SEPTA trains. Bikes and scooters are also a common sight throughout the city.
